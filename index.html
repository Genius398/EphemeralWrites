Ephemeral Writes

Our aim

To develop and seek acquaintances with skilled and growing writers. To soar high in the world of poetry by a union of willing poets, along with a great oppurtunity for socializing and enhancing writing skills. To fold hidden talents out for the good and enrich their passions and seemingly trivial hobbies with love and support and give people a safe space to nurture their skills.




Our schedule

Set in routine, all members will be provided a scheduled meeting timetable each week, enlisting the different activities that will take place. The meetings will be online/offline according to availabity on weekends.

An example of these activities is - Making our own muses by getting inspiration from drawings that we make, coloring designs and coming up with themes for poems using those designs. Each week will have a verbal discussion on those poems and the best ones will get media recognition after approval.




Future plans

As a club, we for sure have some ideas in mind to improve the working of our club in the future. We hope to engage more audience and be the hosts of something extraordinary. Some of these things can be open mics, poetry submissions and such.

Obviously not everybody can be a part of a club, but we hope to make it open and share beautiful works with society and even include them in some fun activities!! Afterwards, we might even have bigger authoritive goals (hint hint) so drop a follow if you're interested in watching youth unfold!
