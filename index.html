Our aim

To develop and seek acquaintances with skilled and growing writers. To soar high in the world of poetry by a union of willing poets, along with a great oppurtunity for socializing and enhancing writing. To fold hidden talents out for the good and enrich their passions and seemingly trivial hobbies with love and support.
